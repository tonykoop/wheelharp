Photorealistic studio product photo of a Wheelharp-style controlled-friction keyboard instrument, with a semicircular keyboard in front, a visible bank of small rosined bowing wheels behind it, strings routed so each key can engage one string against its assigned rotating wheel, and separate dampers poised for clean release. Show guarded wheel edges, service panels, quiet motor-drive housing, adjustable key-action modules, and a refined wood-and-metal prototype frame. Clean studio background, soft directional lighting, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

Natural light, rosin cloth, wheel truing tool, small motor guard, and service checklist nearby, realistic engineering prototype atmosphere.

## Detail Prompt

Shallow depth of field, high detail, realistic wood, felt, metal, and string surfaces.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid text, watermarks, logos, piano hammers, unguarded spinning parts near fingers, impossible wheel/string overlap, extra limbs, distorted hands, and fantasy keyboard layouts unrelated to the semicircular concept.
